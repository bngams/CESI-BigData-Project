# Part 1 — Project Context

## Introduction

The healthcare sector is undergoing a major digital transformation driven by the massive potential of medical data. Practitioners (physicians, nursing staff) and facility administrators need direct access to **actionable information** in order to measurably improve performance and quality of care.

This need has given rise to the demand for scalable IT systems capable of loading, retrieving, and processing medical data through massively parallel processing and distributed resources — enabling complex transformations and analyses to optimise decision-making.

To support this, new aggregated, consolidated, historical, and summarised data architectures are required across multiple domains. This is precisely what modern Big Data infrastructures are designed for, whether through **Data Warehouse** or **Data Lakehouse** systems.

---

## Project Context

Like the rest of the hospital sector, the **HDF (Healthcare Data Flow)** group has recognised the value — and necessity — of a major digital transformation. Your team has been asked to help design and implement its own data warehouse, enabling the group to leverage the vast amounts of data generated by its care management and FTP systems.

### Objectives

- Build a system that collects, stores, and analyses healthcare data from multiple sources
- Connect and unify all data sources into a single pipeline
- Understand what doctors, nurses and hospital managers actually need from the data
- Recommend the right tools — and make sure everything is secure

> Given the confidential nature of the data, the entire infrastructure must be administered under high security, in full compliance with applicable regulations.

## User Needs — Analysis Requirements

The following analyses have been identified through initial consultation with users:

- Patient **consultation rate** at a given facility over a period of time
- Patient consultation rate by **diagnosis** over a period of time
- Overall patient **hospitalisation rate** over a given period
- Hospitalisation rate by **diagnosis** over a given period
- Hospitalisation / consultation rate by **gender** and **age**
- Consultation rate by **professional category**
- Number of **deaths by region** (year 2019)
- Overall **patient satisfaction rate by region** (year 2020)

# Part 2 — Project Tasks

Pick **3 user needs** from the [User Needs — Analysis Requirements](#user-needs--analysis-requirements) section above and implement the full pipeline using modern Big Data tooling.

1. **Architecture** — Define the global system architecture: components, data flows, and technology choices.

2. **Data Integration** (HDFS + NiFi / Python) — Ingest raw data and store it in an intermediate format on HDFS (**Silver Layer**).

3. **EDA + Exploratory Processing** (Spark / Python) — Explore the data to understand its structure, quality, and key trends.

4. **Data Modelling** (Dimensional Schema) — Design a star/snowflake oriented model (or even a constellation one) based on your EDA findings.

5. **Final Data Processing** (Hive — Facts & Dimensions — Data Marts) — Transform and load clean data into final tables in Hive (**Gold Layer**).

6. **(BONUS) Data Viz or ML Training on the Gold layer** — Build dashboards or train a model on top of your Gold data.
